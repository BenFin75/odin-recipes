Banana Bread Recipe

Description

This is a great recipe for Banana Bread. My mom would often make this recipe when I was young, and its always a great option for an easy and quick comfort food that gets better as over time.

Ingredients:

  • Flour - 1 3/4 Cups
  • Baking Powder - 1 1/4 teaspoons
  • Baking Soda - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Sugar - 2/3 Cup
  • Shortening - 1/3 Cup
  • Eggs - 2
  • Milk - 2 Tablespoons
  • Mashed banana - 1 Cup

Recipe:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Beat together sugar and shortening in a medium bowl.
  4. Mix eggs, milk, and bananas into the sugar and shortening mixture.
  5. Add wet ingredents into the dry and mix until combined but still lumpy.
  6. Pour into na oiled loaf pan and smooth top.
  7. cook until a knife inserted into the middle of the loaf comes out clean.
